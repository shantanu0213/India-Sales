Harrow International School BENGALURU

A coeducational boarding and day school for students aged 11 to 18, Harrow International School Bengaluru brings the excellence of the Harrow Family of Schools to India for the first time.

Located on a magnificent new campus, close to the international airport in the Devanahalli area of Bengaluru, the school combines academic excellence with a deeply holistic education. We develop key leadership attributes, self-confidence and intellectual curiosity within our students, through an exhaustive range of academic and cocurricular opportunities.
