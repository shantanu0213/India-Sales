WHAT WE DO.

Vowels of the People Association (VOPA) provides joyous, meaningful education with self-respect and equal opportunity.

We engage in four interventions as follows.

V-School: Liberating Education

V-School is our ‘one million club’ app that provides free digital education for 1st to 10th, Marathi, Urdu, and Semi English Medium students in Maharashtra.

read more

Empowering NIPUN

VOPA’s tech has helped multiple districts implement customized FLN and LO improvement projects impacting 1.2 Lakh children with monthly assessments and analyzing 10 million+ data points.

read more

School Transformation

School level intervention focusing on – Teachers Training, Lesson Planning, Digitizing, School Management etc.

Mental Health for All

Our 2nd App MYCA aims to revolutionize access to free metal health awareness, education, and services at scale.
