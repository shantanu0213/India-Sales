FAQs

What is the curriculum followed at Footprints?

Footprints follows the Highscope curriculum, which is research-based and designed to foster holistic development in children.

How does Footprints ensure the safety of children?

Footprints prioritizes the safety of children by implementing measures such as covered edges, soft cushioned flooring, background checks for staff, and maintaining an all-female staff environment.

Can parents monitor their children while at Footprints?

Yes, parents can monitor their children through live CCTV feed accessible via their mobile devices, providing peace of mind throughout the day.

What policies does Footprints have regarding payments and services?

Footprints offers the flexibility of refunds in case parents are not satisfied with the service. Additionally, parents can manage payments, pause services, or change programs conveniently through the Footprints app.

How does Footprints support children's development?

Footprints provides a nurturing environment where children engage in active learning experiences guided by qualified and experienced staff. The Highscope curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, social interaction, and problem-solving skills essential for lifelong learning.

What are the age groups accepted at Footprints?

Footprints caters to children from infancy through preschool age, providing age-appropriate programs and activities tailored to each developmental stage.

How can parents stay informed about their child's progress at Footprints?

Footprints maintains open communication channels with parents through regular updates, parent-teacher meetings, and progress reports to ensure parents are actively involved in their child's development journey.
