Hey there! I'm Gantavya Sehgal, the founder of YatraBazaar.  As a student, the travel bug bit me hard.  Exploring new cultures and hidden corners of the world was a dream, but expensive tours and tight budgets always seemed to get in the way.  This frustration sparked the idea for YatraBazaar.  I knew there had to be a way to make travel accessible, especially for students like me, while also being responsible for the places we visit.

That's why YatraBazaar isn't just about budget-friendly packages.  We're passionate about creating unforgettable experiences that are kind to our planet and support local communities.  We craft itineraries that minimize our environmental impact and promote cultural preservation.  Think of it as exploring with a conscience!  But don't worry, these values never compromise the affordability or excitement of your adventure.

So, remember that feeling of wanting to explore but being held back by finances? YatraBazaar can turn that dream into reality.  Join me on an unforgettable adventure – let's explore the world together, responsibly and affordably!”
