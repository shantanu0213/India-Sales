At JKLU, dreams aren't just imagined; they're nurtured into reality.

JKLU Laureate Award

JKLU Laureate Award is hosted every year to honour those who have excelled in their field as an example to those who hope to walk their path.

Design Guru Day

The annual ritual by the design community that honours global design thinkers who have significantly made a difference through their ideas and work.

Management Guru Award

Recognising leaders from the world of business for their transformative contribution towards socio-economic growth and inspiring future leaders to follow their footsteps.

Convocation and Founder's Day

The Annual Convocation and Founder's Day felicitates our students on academic achievements and completion of their degree programme.

Upcoming Events

April 17th– 22nd | 2023

Jaipur Design Week

Sabrang, the flagship annual festival  of JKLU will be held on campus from 27th to 29th October, 2023. The theme of the 12th edition of the annual techno-cultural-management-design fest is “Metamorphosis” where the students will celebrate and rejoice in their transition to the outside world as young responsible adults.

April 22nd - 23rd | 2023

Hack JKLU 2.0, JKLU's annual hackathon

HACK JKLU 2.0, the university’s annual hackathon event will be held from 22nd-23rd April, 2023. The event is open to all students with an interest in coding and technology.

Spotlight

Kartavya Anadkat - BCA (2nd year)

Won the 2nd position in the FIDE World Amateur Chess Championship 2023 (OpenU2300) on November 11th in Muscat, Oman. The event saw participation from players across 64 countries.
*Star Achiever*

Deeksha Dixit - B.Tech. (CSE)

Winner of Techfest Competition in Computational Agriculture organised by IIT Mumbai

Afreen Gani Faridi - Assistant Professor (Institute of Management)

Speaker on ‘Empowering and Securing the Futures of Tribal Children: Engendering Child Labour Legislation’. Co-speaker on Human Mobility in ‘Modern Societies: Struggle, Transformation and Critique’. 

Dr. Azeemah Saleem - Assistant Professor (CCCT)

Presented papers on ‘Impact of Visual-Audio Aids on Student Oriented Pedagogy’; ‘The Question of Democratic Values in Turkey’; ‘Social Media and the Voice of Palestinian Women Activist’. Fellow at Centre for Studies of Plural Societies. India Fellow at Islam and Liberty Network, Malaysia.

Sunandini Bansal, Paras Goyal, Ashmit, and A. Saxena

Shamyuktha San - B.Des. (3rd year)
