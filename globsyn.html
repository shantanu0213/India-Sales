Welcome to Globsyn Business School, the best B-School in Kolkata

Over the last two decades, Globsyn Business School (GBS) has not only positioned itself amongst the best business schools in Kolkata but also as India’s first ‘Corporate B-School’, with its two years full-time AICTE approved Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) & Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) – Business Analytics, and MBA (Global) programme in collaboration with Malaysia University of Science & Technology. We have received many laurels from the education fraternity from time to time and have been ranked among the Top 5 B-Schools in Eastern India, as per a recent Business India’s Best B-Schools Survey.
