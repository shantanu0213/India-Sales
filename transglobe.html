About Trans Globe

Know more

Incepted in 1992, Trans Globes humble beginnings incorporated International and Domestic Travel Solutions for all and many. Transiting to Abroad Education and IELTS Coaching was the need of the hour.

Trans Globe leads the race in serving the best in Overseas Education. Headquartered in Rajkot, Gujarat, this 32+ years of experience is our rich legacy and proof that over time we continue winning peoples faith by successfully accomplishing their desires to study abroad as an international student. Our numbers speak volumes, with a 98.4% visa approval ratio, and 50,000 plus student enrolments, Trans Globe has a network of 20+ operational offices in India, Australia and Nepal.

Trans Globe Partnered with the worlds top universities and we have a panel of more than 100 trained advisors besides the supporting executives across our offices providing a world of academic options and handpicked courses to aspiring students. Our successful prompt and highly comprehensive guidance to our students stand as testimony to our phenomenal growth. However, it doesnt happen in a cinch, our blood tears, and sweat along with students and parents faith, helped transform dreams into magical realities. We have partnered with 850+ educational institutes globally to build a network of smart, diverse, and goal-oriented industry professionals that helps aspirants grow in Australia, UK, USA, Canada, Europe, New Zealand, Dubai, and Singapore.

In coming years, we envision, Trans Globe fostering education globally, in every corner of the country guiding ambitious students like true friends with the worlds best mentors and a go-to solution aiming at world-class universities abroad. With my teams collective support and dedication, in integration with our philosophy of working in Students Interest First, we aim to let every student prepare for a brilliant career overseas and parents dream big.
