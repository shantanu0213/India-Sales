About SAAR

The word SAAR is inspired by the phrase Gita Saar which means the Essence of the Bhagavad Gita. The crux of this Hindu scripture is "Change is the law of nature".

Education is ever-evolving, requiring modern methods. SAAR Education embraces the 21st-century approach, aligning with the National Education Policy (NEP) and National Curriculum Framework (NCF). We focus on holistic development through multidisciplinary curricula and extracurricular activities. Our international collaborations offer a global perspective and cultural sensitivity. We guide educators in implementing essential changes, preparing learners for the future.

Our products are classroom-tested, research-based, & standards-aligned.
