About ISBR Business School

ISBR Business School, one of the top business schools in India takes pride in creating an atmosphere wherein both students and faculty can pursue boundless knowledge; a single roof where theory and practice go hand in hand and lead to a better understanding of oneself and the world. Education at ISBR prepares you to think boldly and act confidently in any business environment. At ISBR the focus goes beyond education. ISBR aims to prepare students for life - transforming them into Leaders.

The success of ISBR as an acknowledged institution for quality learning is an outcome of the various facets that give ISBR its characteristic value - the faculty, enterprising students, infrastructure facilities and industry partnerships.

We welcome you to be a part of this ranked institution.

Placements @ ISBR

Placement has been the core strength of ISBR with its strategic location.

ISBR has developed into one of India's leading business schools with visionary leadership as well as the support of a large number of corporate. As a premier business school guided by a Business Advisory Council consisting of industry stalwarts and an Academic Advisory Council comprising foremost management thinkers, ISBR has a constantly updated industry-relevant curriculum which helps nurture an exceptional pool of students in to 'Business Ready' managers. The testimony to the quality of the programs and the talent pool has been the long lasting relationships with an ever growing pool of marquee recruiters.

Companies across all sectors have recruited ISBR students. Our alumni are currently employed with companies like Wipro, Infosys, Accenture, Citibank, Dell, Google, Reliance Industries, Bharti Group, GE, Goldman Sachs, Decathlon, Volvo, Daimler, HCL, HDFC Bank, IBM, ICICI Bank, TCS, Godrej Group, Landmark Group, TUV Adecco, TUV Rheinland, Fraunhofer, Flipkart, Amazon, Boeing, Biocon, PwC, Deutsche Bank, Thomson Reuters, Thomas Cook, The Taj Group of Hotels, Prestige Group, Fortis Healthcare and many others.

ISBR has been awarded Platinum Ranking by AICTE-CII survey 2016 and 2017 consecutively for Industry Academia Linkages for being among the top 3% emerging B-Schools in India.

We welcome you to explore how ISBR can support your organization's talent and professional development requirements.

Dear Parents

Welcome to ISBR Business School!

You're here because you're checking out a good institute for your son / daughter. A good business school focuses on the holistic development of its students and this is what makes ISBR stand apart from other B-schools. A perfect blend of theory and practice, overall grooming, providing opportunities to nurture and hone talent beyond academics, sensitizing students towards their responsibilities, towards their fellow human beings and society at large are some of the initiatives we believe in and heavily focus on, making our students not only successful professionals but responsible individuals.
