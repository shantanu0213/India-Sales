ABOUT US

Praise Foundation is a registered not for profit that facilitates social impact projects under the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals by partnering with corporates, organizations, and volunteers. We have worked with over 75+ social welfare projects that fall under causes such as education, health, child rights, women empowerment, urban and rural community development, elderly care, disability, the environment, and animal welfare. Our projects are currently spread across Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Pune, with a scope to expand to more regions.

 

Over the past 8 years, we have been working with 45+ MNC corporates including Microsoft, RR Donnelley, Amazon, Park Hyatt, Altimetrik India,  ANZ, Price Waterhouse and Cooper among others.

CAUSES WE SUPPORT

  • Child rights
    Every child has a right to a childhood that is free of abuse, exploitation and labour. Praise Foundation partners towards the educational, vocational, mental and emotional rehabilitation of children rescued from streets, railway stations, trafficking and the dumping yard in the city of Chennai, Puducherry, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Pune.
  • Differently Abled
    2.1% of the Indian population comprises of Persons with Disabilities (Pwds). Empowering Persons with Disabilities (Pwds) through community awareness, formal and informal education and job placement in Chennai and Bengaluru is a key strategic focus of Praise Foundation.
  • Education
    With overall drop out rate of children standing at 12.6 %, Praise Foundation focuses on sponsoring and providing formal and non-formal education, life skills and vocational training for children in slum communities, child care institutions and rural areas in and around Chennai, Puducherry, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Pune.
  • Elderly
    At 138 billion, the Indian Elderly population is the second highest in the world. To address the growing concerns of elderly isolation and support health care and financial costs, Praise Foundation partners with old age homes in Chennai. The goal is to empower the homeless elderly to live dignified and protected lives.
  • Environment
    As per a study conducted by IIT Madras about two decades ago, an estimated 650 waterbodies existed in Chennai region alone. At present, only a fraction remains and even those are under attack due to over pollution. Praise Foundation in partnership with environmentalists and NGOs works towards restoration of water bodies, lake clean ups, tree planting and beautification of the city on a regular basis.
  • Health & Nutrition
    With the under 5 mortality rate at 32 deaths/1000, India has made considerable stride in improving child health but child survival continues to be a matter of urgent concern. In an attempt to combat malnutrition in children from lower economic backgrounds, Praise Foundation provides a nutritious health supplement to children from government schools and slum communities in Chennai and Bengaluru. The results have been encouraging as children show signs of strength and immunity.
  • Women Empowerment
    A woman’s right to security, mobility and decision making are the three indicators of her empowerment. Praise Foundation partners with organizations to secure the empowerment of women through financial stability, skill training, vocational support and counseling.

HIGHLIGHT PROJECTS

  • CHILD RIGHTS
    Supporting child rights covers a wide scope which includes but is not limited to educational support for underprivileged children, infrastructure support for schools and CCIs, after school classes and life skills training, nutritional support for underprivileged children and those living with HIV/AIDS, sports rehabilitation for shelter homes and supporting the child help desk at railway stations to repatriate runaway/ abandoned children. Read more....
  • DISABILITY
    For Praise Foundation, working with the differently abled involves a spectrum of activities like assistive services in providing developmental devices, sponsoring surgeries, funding occupational and physiotherapy equipment, enabling educational and nutritional support for children with disabilities and supporting skill development for persons with disabilities to name a few. Employee engagement through play therapy and painting of assistive devices are interactive sessions to work with those with mobility impairment. Read more....
  • ENVIRONMENT
    Conservation of our environment through beach and lake clean ups, water body restorations, tree planting, solid waste management, corporate awareness workshops are a few approaches we have employed to address the pressing need of environmental concerns. To enhance our impact, Praise Foundation has hand-picked small to medium sized deserving NPO’s that work for environmental needs. Read more....
  • HEALTH AND NUTRITION
    Studies have proved that adequate and timely nutritional support is crucial in combatting infant and maternal mortality, improving adolescent and elderly health and building immunity for children from disadvantaged backgrounds. Praise Foundation has made concerted efforts in implementing nutritional projects to improve maternal and infant health, enhance overall health for children from urban and rural slums, provide nutritional support for children living with HIV/AIDS and for children and elderly in institutional care. Read more....
  • ELDERLY
    Praise Foundation works towards the upliftment of the elderly through services of counselling, health care and nutritional support. Projects monitoring the health status of the elderly, referrals for those from abandoned backgrounds and planning special events are some of the on-going activities with our partner organisations. Read more....
  • EDUCATION
    Education being a fundamental right for development is the backbone of our social impact projects. Education support with Praise Foundation ranges from providing Digital Literacy and Spoken English classes to youth from urban slum communities, sponsoring school fees for deserving students, providing infrastructure support to government aided schools and conducting after school classes and vocational training for children and youth in urban and rural communities. Read more....
  • WOMEN EMPOWERMENT
    Women empowerment projects with Praise Foundation include skill training for women from rural and slum communities, livelihood support for underprivileged women, rehabilitation and vocational support for survivors of abuse and violence, counselling and rescue services for women and minors. Read more....
