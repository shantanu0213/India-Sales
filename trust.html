FOR SCHOOL EVENTS

Our planetarium will engage, amaze, and inspire as we take you on a Space adventure. Suitable for Primary Schools, High Schools, and Families. Programs include Indigenous Astronomy, Scientific discoveries, Space Technology, The hunt for extraterrestrial life, and more.

FOR SUMMER CAMPS

Tare Zameen Par presents over 50 unique shows for preschool, elementary, middle school and high school kids. Keep your kids learning and having fun all summer long with engaging and inspiring astronomy and space experiences.

FOR SPECIAL EVENTS

Our mobile planetarium can be hired for special events (annual function, family function, Birthday party, etc.). Such as Corporate events organized by MNC Companies and other IT Companies. It's always a thrilled and new experience for their families and friends.

Our Services

Under the brand name Tare Zameen Par Provide following Services

Science Outreach Programs

Our innovative Science outreach Program includes Digital Planetarium Shows, Science experiments demonstration and training.

Teachers Training Programs

The Technology is always gets updates. We train Teachers and introduce them with latest Pedagogy so Students can learn in more effective way.

Quiz Competitions

The Quiz competition like "Kaun Banega Saupati" (Inspired by Television Show Kaun Banega Karodpati). That Brings Joy and Fun While Learning New Things.

Eradication of Superstitious Belief

Even in 21st Century there are Many People Having Fear and Misunderstanding. Our Programs Bring Awareness to Get Rid from Superstitious Belief.

Expert Talk

We Bring Experts from the Institutions like BARC, ISRO etc.... To Interact with Students and Youth. These Programs are Being Conducted Online as well as Offline Mode.
