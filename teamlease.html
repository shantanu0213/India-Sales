What are Entry-level & Blue-collar jobs?
When you hear this term, what is the first question that pops up on your mind? Why are these terms trending and popular nowadays? Entry-level and blue-collar jobs are basically known as the manual workforce. The manual workforce is a primary key factor in industrial sectors such as logistics, food industry, construction, manufacturing, warehousing, agriculture, transportation, beauty, telecom and retail sector, for both entry-level and experienced employees.

There are a huge number of entry-level and blue-collar job vacancies for 9th pass, 10th pass, 12th pass,  diploma, and degree candidates across India. But the percentage of workers or employees placed to companies who are hiring for entry-level and blue-collar workers such as plumbers,  fitters,  mechanics,  electricians,  beauticians,  drivers,  deliveries,  staff nurses,  Data entry,  cooks/chefs,  security,  and maids/nannies are very low. Teamlease.com tries to reduce this gap by matching the right employees to the right job, where workers can check, search, and apply for jobs in their own preferred local languages. This will decrease the recruitment and operational costs of companies towards entry-level and blue-collar jobs, at the same time increase employment in entry-level & blue-collar and vocational sectors, respectively.

Why Teamlease.com for Entry-level & Blue-collar jobs?
Getting placed in an entry-level or blue-collar job market in India is very difficult. If you are looking for entry-level jobs or vocational sector jobs, then you’re in the right place. Teamlease.com is a job portal that helps in finding jobs for workers or employees with ease. TeamLease is considered as one of the best job sites that deal with entry-level and blue-collar jobs in all metropolitan cities such as Bangalore,  Hyderabad,  Mumbai,  Delhi,  Pune,  Chennai,  Coimbatore,  Kolkata,  Surat  and Visakhapatnam. As we are already aware, workers or employees searching for entry-level or blue-collar jobs may not have a proper well-designed resume or college degree to showcase. In order to remove this gap, we introduced TeamLease.com, as a recruitment platform specifically designed for skilled laborers, entry-level employees and experienced entry-level and blue-collar workers, where they can search for jobs and can apply easily for best-in-class entry-level & blue-collar hiring companies across India such as OLA, Swiggy, Zomato, Uber eats, Uber, Box8, Pizza hut, Dominos, KFC, Fasoos, Foodpanda, Takeaway, Justeat, etc.
