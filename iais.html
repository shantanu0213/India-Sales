    Welcome to Indus Altum International School, Belagavi

    Indus Altum International School is situated on a 17-acre campus at Kakati Hobli, Belagavi, the second capital of Karnataka. Situated near the foothills of the Sahyadri mountain range in the Western Ghats at an altitude of 779m, Belagavi (formerly known as Belgaum). Given its strategic proximity to Goa and its salubrious climate, the British chose it as a key cantonment, giving rise to a unique aristocratic atmosphere of its own.

    Consistently ranked among the best international schools in Belgaum, India, the school nurtures the right environment for young leaders to grow, be creative, and culturally sensitive with an international outlook. The school is part of the award winning Indus group of international schools that has made a name for their unique approach to academics in the school years. The school prides itself in its ability to combine the best of traditional values with the best educational practices worldwide, teaching techniques, technology and expert resources. The focus on the holistic development of each student highlights the culture of Indus Altum.

    IAIS draws a student community from all around the world, complemented by the most modern state-of-the-art facilities for the first time at Belagavi. With a teacher/student ratio of 1:9 and a maximum of 25 students per class, IAIS offers a great global educational experience with boarding facilities for its students, which every parent can be proud of.

    Dear Parents and Teachers,

    As we advance into the 21st century, our STEAM-Humanities curriculum firmly places mathematics at its core,recognizing it as the foundational language for interdisciplinary learning. To deepen this approach, we have established a Math Circle guided by four of India’s most reputed mathematicians, providing unparalleled expertise to our students. Furthermore, we are conducting intentional R&D to explore how AI technology can enhance mathematical mindedness, aiming to elevate students’ conceptual understanding and application of math in innovative ways.Together, we are building the future, one equation at a time.
