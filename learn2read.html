Intermediate

Active Readers

Active Readers’ Course is designed for children aged 4 to 6 years! Led by expert teachers and phonics specialists, this course makes learning fun and engaging. With 66 classes over 22 weeks (three classes per week), your child will dive into reading, sentence formation, and writing. Small group sessions ensure personalized attention, with each batch consisting of only 3-4 children. By the end of the course, your child will confidently read and write up to 8-letter words, enjoy age-appropriate storybooks independently, and develop a love for reading. Join us on this exciting learning journey!

Beginner

Junior Readers PLUS

Our Junior Readers' PLUS Course, designed for children aged 3.5 to 4 years, offers a dynamic learning experience led by expert teachers and phonics specialists. With 48 classes over 4 months, held three times a week in small groups of 3-4 children, personalized attention is guaranteed. Through engaging activities, children master fundamental language skills like letter sounds, blending, and basic reading concepts. This comprehensive approach not only fosters reading and writing abilities but also enhances memory and retention skills, preparing them for future academic success. Advanced

Power Readers

The Power Readers’ Course is designed for advanced learners aged 5 to 7 years. Led by teaching professionals and phonics experts, this course focuses on enhancing reading, writing, and comprehension skills. Over 20 weeks and 60 classes, children will strengthen their foundation in English while exploring stories and books. With small class sizes of 3-4 children, each child receives personalized attention to read independently, understand stories, and share their own ideas. Through engaging activities, this course stimulates imagination, boosts confidence, and fosters a lifelong love for reading.

Advanced

Super Readers

Our Super Readers Course is designed for kids aged 6 to 8 years. Led by teaching professionals and phonics experts, this course is tailored for advanced learners who have already mastered the basics of English, like letters and phonics sounds. In this course, children will progress to reading, writing, and understanding stories and novels. Through engaging activities, we stimulate their minds and help them build a strong foundation in language. By the end of the course, children will not only excel in reading but also enjoy creating and sharing their own stories with friends.
