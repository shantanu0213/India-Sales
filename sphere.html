Spherule Foundation

Improving Life, One Breath At a Time

Spherule Foundation an awardee of Global Icon Award 2022, G20 Summit for social work, India CSR Award 2022, Best NGO and Most Trusted 30 NGOs of India Award 2022 and more

Started in 2017 with a vision to work primarily to bring deep-rooted sustainable change in people’s lives, vision is to create an equal, just and sustainable society. People should have a right to life and security, to a sustainable livelihood, to be heard, to have an identity and to have access to basic social services. We believe that with the necessary action and political will, this world is possible.

Certification and Empanelments

Spherule Foundation is TISS (Tata Institute Of Social Sciences) Empanelled NGO.TISS empanelled NGOs are considered as Professional pool of development sector organizations in India and eligible to be part of National CSR Hub.NGOs that are fulfilling the parameters of credibility and competency decided by TISS get empanelled based on Financial capacity, Transparency, Sustainability, Governance & Structure of Organization, Core Activity, Strategy, Methodology, Staff, Administrative Policies and Processes of NGO.
