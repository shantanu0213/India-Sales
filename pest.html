A Pest Control Company

Seva Facility Services Private Limited, a pest control company established in March 2016 by Hiraman Rathod, who is Msc Agriculture with specialization in Plant Pathology with 30 + years’ experience in service industry. Company provide various pest control services to residential, commercial, industrial segment. Accredited for fumigation with methyl bromide as per NSPM 11/12 and ISPM 15 International standard, including Australian consignment – AQIS. Accredited for fumigation with aluminium phosphide as per NSPM 22 for export container & commodity fumigation. Apart from pest control and fumigation service we have separate set up for water tank cleaning, housekeeping and security services for our esteemed customers. We have dedicated expert team for each section. Company is having 25000+ satisfied customers across India in different segment. Head Office is at Pune, Maharashtra, India. Branches at Mumbai, Aurangabad, Amravati, Nagpur, Belgaum, Indore,Hyderabad and Nizamabad. For more details kindly visit service page which you are looking for.
