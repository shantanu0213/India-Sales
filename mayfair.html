ABOUT THE BRAND

More than four decades ago Shri Dilip Ray had a dream of setting up world-class hotels and resorts that would establish a benchmark in the world of hospitality, with its trademark low-rise structures, heavily aesthetic in its appeal, and ensconced within a biodiverse eco-friendly environment, where vibrant and welcoming interiors combined with warm hospitality would provide guests with the comfort of a home away from home.

IN THE SPOTLIGHT

AMID LUSH GREENERY

Mayfair is uniquely designed to be within an ecological biosphere of its own, in sync with nature where the sheer lush flora creates an ambience of peace and tranquillity. An experience worth savouring for moments where the sound of the gentle crashing of waves will lull you to peaceful slumber and where the rustling leaves and chirping of birds will wake you up to the perfect dawn.

A HERITAGE VIBE

Restoring the old-world charm is what MAYFAIR is about! Our Hotels & Resorts are adorned with treasured objet d'art like vintage cars, statues and exquisite artefacts. We commemorate art and history through a series of paintings that embellish our hallways, giving guests a peek into the captivating Indian and Odia history.

WELLNESS AND REJUVENATION

Each of our properties is decked with an in-house wellness spa to help you heal and untie your knots! Our team of professionals curate a regime of therapies and treatments to cure your fatigue. Rejuvenate the MAYFAIR way!

MORE FROM MAYFAIR

VINTAGE CHARM

Our heritage hotels have inherited unique legacies, where folklore and history are etched in every corner and the symbolic representation of Vintage decors & artefacts carry memories of Yore.

A HISTORICAL TOUCH

We are proud to be Odia and aim to commemorate the culture. We have preserved the Aircraft D 185 Twin Beech, which was owned by the great Maharaja of Mayurbhanj. It was the first aircraft owned by an Odia, a splendour that is still prevalent today.

ARTS & ARTEFACTS

MAYFAIR is not a regular hospitality brand. We boast of making a unique experience. Each corner and corridor of MAYFAIR are adorned with fascinating sculptures and paintings that are inspired by the rich history of Odisha.
