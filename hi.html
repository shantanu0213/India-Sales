FAQs

  1. What is pest control?

    2. Pest control is a regulated process to control different pest species such as cockroaches, mosquitoes, rodents, termites, bed bugs, and wood borers. The best pest control service is one that is conducted using eco-friendly or herbal pest control solutions.


  2. What are the 3 principles of pest control?

    3. The 3 principles of pest control are:

    1. Prevention – Maintaining hygiene to prevent pest infestation
    2. Suppression – Reducing the damage done by pest infestation to an acceptable level
    3. Eradication – Killing the pest population on a premise

  3. What are the 4 types of pests?

    4. Pests are tiny organisms that can pose a threat to our health and property. There are different types of pests found around us. The four most common types of pests are cockroaches, rats, mosquitoes, and termites.
