WELCOME TO DHAULADHAR HEIGHTS RESORT

Your Comfort, Our Craft – Discover Your Perfect Stay!

Welcome to Dhauladhar Heights Resort Dharamshala! Nestled amidst the breathtaking Dhauladhar mountain range, our resort offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Experience the perfect blend of modern comfort and nature’s beauty as you unwind in our luxurious accommodations, each designed to provide an unforgettable stay.

Indulge in a wide array of amenities, from our inviting pool and spa facilities to our delectable dining options that cater to every palate. Whether you’re seeking adventure or relaxation, our resort has something to offer for every traveler.
