Our Technology

Our Linear Fresnel Reflector (LFR) and Multiple Effect Distillation (MED) desalination systems are effective and efficient technologies that help us to provide innovative and cost-effective clean energy and pure water solutions. Our LFR technology is a low-cost, high-quality system that requires minimal land, while our MED system is an energy-efficient process that is able to handle complex feed water.
