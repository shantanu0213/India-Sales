Choice Based Credit System

Read More

The Neotia University (TNU) follows the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) model that offers students to opt for inter / intradisciplinary courses and subjects of their choice depending upon their interests and goals – Core, Departmental Elective, and University General Elective. The CBCS grading pattern is based on earned credits every semester.

Scholarships

read more

We believe that no student should be stopped from pursuing their passion or achieving their full potential because of financial difficulty. At TNU, we offer numerous merit-based as well as need-based scholarships to ensure that no student is left behind. There is no limit to the number of scholarships granted by the University and if a student is eligible as per the given criteria then he or she will get the “Guaranteed Scholarship”.

  • Chancellor Scholarship (As decided by the Empowered Committee upto 100%)
  • Gramotthan Scholarship
  • Sarvodaya Scholarship
  • Sports Scholarship
  • Mere Apne Scholarship
  • Cultural Scholarship
  • Sahodarya Scholarship
  • Special Hill Students Scholarship
  • Industry Sponsorship
