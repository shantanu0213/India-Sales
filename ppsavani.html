PPSU 1st Convocation

P P Savani University celebrated its 1st Convocation ceremony on Thursday, December 24, 2020, via the online platform. The event was graced by Dr. Jacqueline Fletcher, Regent Professor, Emerita, Oklahoma State University, and Mr. Atik M. Desai, Vice President & Chief Administrative Officer, Hazira Central Services, Larsen & Turbo Limited, along with Vallabhbhai Savani, President, P P Savani University, Members of Governing Body of P P Savani University, Dr. Parag Sanghani, Provost, Registrar, trustees of PPSU, Principals, Staff members & students.
The highlight of the ceremony was performing traditional rituals of dikshant havan as per ancient Vedic tradition at the convocation ceremony which impressed everyone at the ceremony and gave a message to stay connected with your roots and values despite adapting to modernization.
The convocation ceremony witnessed the conferment of 7 Gold Medals &162 Degrees to the students of Bachelor of Science, Bachelor of Interior Design, and Bachelor of Business Administration.
