Meals

The raw material for the meals is picked from the surrounding farms to ensure highest level of freshness. All the meals are served in the beautifully curated dining areas to give experience of camping style. Three meals i.e. Breakfast, Lunch and Evening snack which are tasty and hygienic are served at fixed timings.

Activities

Experience adventure sports like Rock Climbing, Zipline, Zorbing, Rope Courses, amongst many other activities. Campers can quickly scramble up the mountains for a short trek, explore villages close by or even indulge in some rural activities like camel cart rides or tractor rides.

These kinds of experiences are sure to make memorable experiences for our participants while also building their confidence, will power, focus and leadership skills, in addition to bringing them closer to nature.

Enjoy some quality time with your family in the lap of nature at Bengaluru with a fantastic combination of indoor and outdoor activities. Rejuvenate with age appropriate activities that boost the physical and mental health of every family member and get a chance to bond  like never before.

Get your colleagues and work teams outside from the four walls of the office, for an outbound team-bonding experience at our camp site. With our interesting activities and challenging tasks, your teams would get an opportunity to bond and work collaboratively.

Education has never been restricted to the confines of a school and its four walls. Got a question?

Q1. What should I carry?

All the guests are suggested to wear a face mask and carry government ID proof, hand sanitizer, water bottle and be in sports attire (Track pants and t-shirts). In Indian clothing we suggest females wear Salwar Suite, guests in Sari will not be allowed to participate in activities. We also suggest you to carry carry- sun cap as we will be outdoors the whole day. 

Q2. Are Rocksport programs Covid safe?

Yes, All Rocksport programs are 100% covid safe if the participants follow the instructions laid out by us. There are strict protocols and procedures in place to keep both the participants and the staff Covid safe. To know more about how Rocksport programs are covid safe (read this).

Q3. I have never done adventure sports before. Should i still book it?

Rocksport programs are perfect for the beginners. We are also your first step to adventure. Our programs are designed in such a way that all ages and all levels enjoy the day as it’s not about the activities but the whole experience. You should definitely join the program.

Q4. How safe is the program?

Rocksport programs are 100% safe. We have delivered 100% error free programs in the past decade now. Over 15 lakh participants have experienced our program right from kids from school to families and some of the top leaders of the corporate world.
