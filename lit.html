What is LIT & how does it stand out?

- LIT redefines learning by integrating real brands into the classroom.
- Students solve real-world problems, gain hands-on experience, and receive feedback from industry professionals.
- Ensures students acquire practical and market-ready skills.

What are the courses that LIT offers?

- Creator Marketer: 1 year program focused on storytelling, community building, and social media.
- Creatorpreneur: 2 year program blending marketing and business skills.

Does LIT offer placement assistance?

- Yes, LIT provides 100% placement assistance to students.

What is the eligibility criteria?

- Minimum qualification: Class 12th pass-out.
- Specific eligibility requirements vary by program.

How does LIT accommodate outstation students?

- The Admin team helps in finding suitable housing/PG for a comfortable learning environment.

Does LIT offer scholarships?

- Scholarships of 5%-15% through the LITMUS Test Challenge, awarded for creativity, strategy, and analytical thinking.

What is the enrolment process?

Step 1: Submit your application.
Step 2: Await shortlisting.
Step 3: Appear for Interview.
Step 4: Upon selection, pay the token amount and engage in the Scholarship Challenge.
Step 5: Begin your LIT journey.

Tuition and Financial Aid

- Information available in the LIT brochure or upon inquiry.

Does LIT offer online courses?

- LIT plans to offer experiential learning programs online in the near future.
