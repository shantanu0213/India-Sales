Current Happenings

Thuvakkam Pongal

Thuvakkam celebrated our Thuvakkam Pongal 2025 at the Andapattu Village in Villupuram District
on 10th Jan 2025, with fun filled games along with the Community and the Farmers along with our
CSR partner Light &amp; Wonder, inaugurating the project board for our Bee Apiary Project.

Thuvakkam Volunteer Summit 2025

Starting the year in style, Thuvakkam hosted the first-ever Volunteer Summit on Jan 4th at the iconic Madras School of Social Work, Chennai, marking International Volunteer Day in style!

with thought-provoking discussions featuring corporate leaders, non-profit pioneers, and government officials sharing their insights on impactful volunteering. This dynamic summit wasn’t just an event; it was a movement, bringing together changemakers, aspiring volunteers, and
dreamers.

Treecember Inauguration

We are excited to share the successful launch of Thuvakkam’s Treecember Campaign at Andapattu village, Villupuram district, on 10th January 2025, as part of our Pongal celebrations. Our goal is to plant and nurture 500,000 indigenous tree saplings across India by World Environment Day 2025. To mark the inauguration, 150 farmers from Andapattu received 150 tree saplings, symbolizing the start of this nationwide effort.

Thuvakkam’s Expansion site visits

Thuvakkam team had a field visit to Rajasthan as a part of our expansion strategy in order to identify aspirational regions in the state. With the assistance of Mr.Ravindra Singh Bhati, Hon’ MLA of Sheo Assembly, we visited various remoted regions of Sheo, in Barmer district.
