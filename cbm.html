Committed to build an inclusive India for persons with disabilities!

About 26.8 million people in India live with disabilities, and they need your support now more than ever before to break the cycle of poverty and disability.

 

Act Now

LEAVE NO ONE BEHIND

Last year we reached 32,99,940 people, including 99,045 persons with disabilities, through our interventions in health, education, livelihood, and disaster response across India.

Why Do We Exist?

CBM India exist to build an inclusive society where persons with disabilities can achieve their full potential.

For over 50 years, CBM India had worked alongside persons with disabilities in some of the poorest communities across India. By working directly with persons with disabilities, other disadvantaged populations, and partner organizations, by influencing policy at all levels, and by inclusively responding to emergencies and natural disasters, we strive to remove barriers that hinder a person’s full and equal participation in society.

Know More
Why Persons with Disabilities?

About 26.8 million people in India live with disabilities!

Disability is both a cause and consequence of poverty. Poverty causes disability due to limited access to good nutrition, adequate health care, clean water, or unsafe working conditions. A person with disability is less likely to have access to education, health and employment and rehabiliation making it difficult for them to escape the vicious cycle of poverty.

Inclusive education

We work towards Education for All (EFA), a fundamental policy where no child is denied access to quality education. We enable mainstreaming of children with disabilities from different socio-economic backgrounds to regular schools. We work on the premise that all children should learn and play together regardless of their impairments and different abilities.

Inclusive health

We work towards building sustainable and inclusive health systems and services for all. We work to minimise the conditions which lead to disability and reduce the prevalence of diseases that cause impairments. Our emphasis is that health services should be inclusive and accessible to people with disabilities.

Inclusive livelihood

We work towards removing the barriers that limit people with disabilities from participating in livelihood activities. We develop and execute locally relevant on-farm, off-farm and non-farm livelihood systems, which are unique in their approach; bringing together persons with and without disabilities working as one inclusive team.

Humanitarian response

We work towards reducing the impact of disasters on affected communities by mitigating and reducing risks. We aim to equally reach and actively involve persons with disabilities, addressing specific needs while promoting full inclusion in mainstream services. We respond to all major humanitarian crises across India.
