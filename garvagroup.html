About Us​

Journey Of Garva Group

For almost two decades, the real estate industry has had great success. The most crucial point to make is that, during these two decades, the so-called growth has only been limited to areas close to major cities. The development shifted to the following radius as the area became congested.

The Garva Group has withstood the trend and assumption that urban areas are the only places where real estate needs emerge. Also, our worldview is informed by the notion of bringing inner peace to all individuals, not only those who reside in cities.

Nearly two decades after our company's founding, Garva Group has continued to focus on creating places and destinations outside of urban centres. This idea inspired us to explore and create enriching locations in South Mumbai and South Pune.

Garva Group continues to help people own their inner peace by delivering residential projects and land development projects in advantageous and tranquil settings.
