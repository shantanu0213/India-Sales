OUR ROADMAP
FOR SUCCESS

At Sparsh Global Business School, we believe that education is more than just the acquisition of knowledge and skills. Our philosophy is rooted in the idea that education should be transformative, empowering students to become leaders who will shape the future.

With a vision to promote conscious capitalism and sustainable living through transformative business education, we champion the philosophy of 4Ps to nurture purpose driven, environmentally conscious business leaders of tomorrow through curricula design, interventions and initiatives in all the programmes we offer.

4P
PHILOSOPHY
AT SGBS

PURPOSE

our students follow to remain environment friendly at all times through innovation and adaptability to market sensibilities.

PLANET

recognizes that businesses are part of a larger ecosystem and must take into account the impact of operations on the environment and society.

PEOPLE

students are taught that by investing in the well-being of their employees businesses can create a positive and supportive workplace culture that contributes to employee satisfaction and retention

PROFIT

maintaining a competitive edge through superior products and continuing to generate profits that fuel further growth and investment.

EXPERIENTIAL LEARNING Learning through doing, experiencing, and reflecting.

ENTREPRENEURIAL SPIRIT A mindset of innovation, creativity, and risk-taking.

EXPERIMENTAL APPROACH Approaching problems with a mindset of experimentation and iteration.

ENVIRONMENTAL CONSCIOUSNESS A commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility.

ADVANTAGE

At SPARSH, our residential Undergraduate and Graduate Programmes are tailored for students who are eager to go the extra mile to prepare for a successful future. Our programs are thoughtfully designed by leading experts in both academia and industry, with a strong foundation in core management subjects such as Marketing, HR, Finance, Strategy, Supply Chain, and Operations. In addition, we offer an array of cutting-edge courses that cover emerging technologies, design thinking, artificial intelligence, data science and analytics, and sustainable development. By providing students with this holistic education, we equip them with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in today’s fast-paced and ever-changing business world.
