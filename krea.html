Creativity in Action

The Krea Edge

Krea University is instituted by a team of global academicians, industrialists and intellectuals with a vision for the 21st Century and beyond. Guided by a unique Interwoven Learning model, our programmes encourage creativity, critical thinking, communication, contemplation, cross-disciplinary approaches, real-world implementation, and the ability to negotiate unanswered questions. At Krea University, students are guided by some of the best academic minds drawn from leading universities around the world, a combination of path-breaking young scholars to highly experienced experts. Krea University will shape a new generation of adaptable, resilient leaders, giving them critical thinking skills and the ethical grounding needed to forge ahead.

Schools and Centres

Krea University currently houses two Schools and provides strategic oversight to several Research Centres

  • The BA, BSc, BA (Honours), BSc (Honours) and PhD degrees offered at the School of Interwoven Arts and Sciences (SIAS), Krea University equip our students with confidence, purpose, and the capacity for creative and critical thinking
  • IFMR Graduate School of Business (GSB) offers 5 year Integrated BBA + MBA, 2 year MBA, PhD, and customised Management Development programmes, including Executive Education programmes
  • The Research Centres LEAD, J-PAL South Asia, CDFI, Inclusion Economics India Centre, Moturi Satyanarayana Centre for Advanced Study in the Humanities and Social Sciences, Sapien Labs Centre for the Human Brain and Mind, and Water, Environment, Land and Livelihoods (WELL) Labs work closely with international agencies, governments, policymakers and academicians in solving complex developmental challenges
