Why RIIM

PGDM College in Pune

Ramachandran International Institute of Management (RIIM), Pune is one of the leading and recognised Management Institutes of India. RIIM is Pune's 1st Corporate Style B-School. It was found under the able guidance of Visionary leader, Educator and First generation Entrepreneur Mr. Suraj Sharma in the year 2014. RIIM was started with the vision to bridge the Industry- Academia gap by making the existing PGDM programmes more Practical, Industry relevant and Skill based. RIIM Pune is approved by AICTE New Delhi, Ministry of Education, Govt. of India.
