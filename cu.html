SEM Lab

One of the most powerful and useful tools for material analysis, SEM or Scanning Electron Microscopy is a test process that scans a sample with an electron beam to produce a magnified image for analysis.This lab facility helps you understand and master various core aspects of the technology.

XRD Lab

Primarily used for phase identification of a crystalline material, X-ray diffraction (XRD) is a rapid analytical technique that can provide information on unit cell dimensions. The XRD Lab helps the students research and practice various aspects of the same.

Mol-Biology Lab
