What We Do

At Pragyanam, we’ve engaged in countless conversations with parents, and beneath their aspirations for their children lies a common desire – happiness and contentment. We are on a dedicated mission at Pragyanam, a CBSE-affiliated institution, to cultivate lasting happiness and contentment in every child. Guided by the World Health Organization’s endorsement of 10 essential life skills that underpin an individual’s ability to find joy and satisfaction in any circumstance, we’ve seamlessly woven these skills into our captivating curriculum. This integration ensures the learning experience transforms into a delightful self-discovery and personal growth voyage. Read More
