Yuva Unstoppable

Yuva Unstoppable is an India & US registered leading NGO founded in 2006 by a group of zealous individuals led by Amitabh Shah with a mission to empower children and youth through various initiatives. Through its grassroot-level programs Yuva Unstoppable today has emerged as one of the leading NGOs in India with initiatives towards education, infrastructure upgradation and health & hygiene.

We are dedicated to creating positive social impact and have a strong presence across India. Our organization works on a variety of issues, including education, healthcare, and the environment, and we have successfully implemented numerous projects and initiatives that have made a real difference in the lives of those we serve. 

If you are passionate about making a difference and want to support our mission, we invite you to join us at Yuva Unstoppable. Together, we can create a brighter future for all
