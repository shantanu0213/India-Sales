About Us

A Vision Rooted In Excellence

Founded in 2007, Tripura Constructions emerged as a visionary force in Hyderabad’s real estate sector. At a time when the city was expanding rapidly, Pasupuleti Sudhakar recognized the need for quality-driven yet affordable housing solutions. What started as a modest venture soon transformed into a trusted name in construction, delivering a perfect balance of affordability, quality, and strategic locations. With a keen focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, Tripura Constructions has become a synonym for trust and excellence, setting new benchmarks in residential and commercial projects.
